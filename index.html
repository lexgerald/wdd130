Sierra Leone

Sierra Leone is a country on the southwest coast of West Africa. It shares its southeastern border with Liberia, and the northern half of the nation is surrounded by Guinea. The country can be divided into four distinct physical regions: the coastal swamp, the Sierra Leone Peninsula, the interior plains, and the interior plateau and mountain region. The highest peak is Mount Bintumani, which is 1,948 meters (6,391 ft) above sea level.
